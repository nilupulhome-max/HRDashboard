🗑 Danger Zone — Delete Records
Option 1 — Clear by Date Range

Permanently deletes all Canteen, OT and Manpower entries within the selected dates. Cannot be undone.

Option 2 — Complete Database Clear

Wipes every document from all data collections (Canteen, OT, Manpower, Attendance, OT Plans, Meal Requests, Employees, Rates, Config, Roster). User accounts are preserved. This is irreversible.
